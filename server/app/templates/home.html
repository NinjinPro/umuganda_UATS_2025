Welcome to UATS - Your Digital Umuganda Assistant

UATS (Umuganda Attendance & Fines Tracking System) is a smart and simple platform built to digitalize how communities organize and manage their monthly Umuganda community programs. From real-time attendance to automated fine management, we make community service more effective, accountable, and transparent.

Why we created UATS ?

  • Easily schedule and manage monthly Umuganda events
  • Track attendance with a few clicks â€” even from your phone
  • Send automated mobile money fines for non-participation
  • Notify citizens with SMS reminders and event details
  • Get accurate community participation reports

How the system Works

  1. Admin or leader logs in and creates an Umuganda Community Session
  2. Citizens receive SMS reminders before the event
  3. On event day, attendance is recorded live or after
  4. The system calculates and notifies those with fines
  5. Reports are generated automatically
